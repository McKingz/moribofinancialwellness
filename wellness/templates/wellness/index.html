Maud Mogodi
Managing Director

Maud is the Founding and Managing Director of Moribo Holding Company. She has a combined experience of over 14 years in banking and the Real Estate and Housing Developments environments, with the majority of this being in the banking industry. Outside the banking industry, Maud has more than 10years of experience in both Community Relations and Marketing and Sales environments. Maud is a qualified Debt Counsellor coupled with qualifications in Communications, Marketing and PR, Project Management – in both the built environment and general projects. Her vast experience ranging from running her own enterprises, to real estate franchising and banking has equipped her to handle multiple entities with flair.
